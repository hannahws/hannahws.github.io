A pop up exhibition exploring the tensions between absence and presence.










Concept

Lost & Found initiates discourse around the interpretation and relationship we have with the world around us. It is an exploration of the human condition through an innate loneliness found within the inanimate and the romantic ability to apply meaning to such.

Clothing as adornment and protection, an introspective engagement with obsolescent communication, and visual interpretation of atmosphere and space.

Garment as necessity. Atmosphere without manifestation. Communication without destination.

Works
Mr McLuhan will see you now...
Finn Chadwick

On display is a sight specific installation consisting of several CRT televisions being displayed through panels of glass which separate two rooms. The immersive experience aims to create an introspective contemplation of the communication medium of television and through this engagement create an awareness of its ever-increasing obsolescence.

The tote bags follow closely with my ethos as a designer whilst using very textured and saturated colours. Pushing the boundaries of taste therefore the viewer is tested in the user experience. A bag is a necessity that holds presence and absence simultaneously.
